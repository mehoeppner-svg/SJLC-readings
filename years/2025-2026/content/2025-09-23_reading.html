September 23: Genesis 4:6-8
▼

6 The LORD said to Cain, “Why are you angry, and why has your face fallen? 7 If you do well, will you not be accepted?1 And if you do not do well, sin is crouching at the door. Its desire is for2 you, and you must rule over it.”

8 Cain spoke to Abel his brother.3 And when they were in the field, Cain rose up against his brother Abel and killed him.

Footnotes

[1] 4:7 Hebrew will there not be a lifting up [of your face]?
[2] 4:7 Or to, or toward, or against (see 3:16)
[3] 4:8 Hebrew; Samaritan, Septuagint, Syriac, Vulgate add Let us go out to the field
