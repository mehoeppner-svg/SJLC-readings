Welcome to Daily Scripture Readings

Join us each day in reading God's Word together as a church community. This resource provides daily Scripture passages to help you grow in faith and connect with the timeless truth of the Gospel.

What This Site Offers

I created this website as a resource for our church community to provide easy access to daily Scripture readings in the ESV (English Standard Version) translation. Whether you're following the Gospel Project curriculum or simply seeking to engage with God's Word each day, you'll find carefully selected passages designed to deepen your understanding of Scripture and strengthen your faith.

Each reading includes:

  • The full Scripture text in ESV translation
  • Beautiful images to accompany the passage
  • Audio narration for listening while you read
  • Commentary and context to enhance understanding
  • Footnotes and cross-references for deeper study

Why Daily Reading Matters

When we read Scripture together as a community, we're united around shared themes and passages that connect us to Jesus Christ and God's redemptive story. Daily engagement with the Bible transforms our hearts, renews our minds, and draws us closer to the living God who speaks to us through His Word.

"All Scripture is breathed out by God and profitable for teaching, for reproof, for correction, and for training in righteousness." — 2 Timothy 3:16

This isn't just about individual Bible study—it's about growing together as the body of Christ, discussing what we've learned, and encouraging one another in our faith journey.

Getting Started

Ready to begin? Click the "Today" link in the navigation menu to access today's Scripture reading. You can also browse previous and upcoming readings by week, or jump to any specific date.

Make it a habit to start each day in God's Word. Set aside 10-15 minutes in the morning or evening to read, reflect, and pray. Consider sharing what you're learning with family, friends, or your small group.
