September 25: Genesis 4:13-16
▼

13 Cain said to the LORD, “My punishment is greater than I can bear.1 14 Behold, you have driven me today away from the ground, and from your face I shall be hidden. I shall be a fugitive and a wanderer on the earth, and whoever finds me will kill me.” 15 Then the LORD said to him, “Not so! If anyone kills Cain, vengeance shall be taken on him sevenfold.” And the LORD put a mark on Cain, lest any who found him should attack him. 16 Then Cain went away from the presence of the LORD and settled in the land of Nod,2 east of Eden.

Footnotes

[1] 4:13 Or My guilt is too great to bear
[2] 4:16 Nod means wandering
